Our Work

Here at StuyPulse, we have a close relationship with Bloomberg Philanthropies, where we work together to staff volunteer events. Over the past year, we have helped immensely by planting trees, restoring parks, picking up garbage, etc. Pulsites also helped to fix park benches and chairs at Governors Island. We look forward to doing more community service work with Bloomberg.
