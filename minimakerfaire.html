Mini Maker Faire

Fall 2017 - Outreach

Our Work

In addition to Maker Faire, every year we help hold Mini Maker Faire. StuyPulse members had a great time with Barnes & Noble Tribeca participating in the Barnes and Noble Mini Maker Faire! Members came and demoed the bot, and talked about the team, STEM, and FIRST. Kids had fun operating the robot and letting it shoot fuel. It was great to see the children have so much fun with Rafael. We are thankful to everyone who supported the team by buying a book!
