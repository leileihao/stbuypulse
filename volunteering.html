Volunteering

StuyPulse believes in giving back to our community! We have spent over 4,000 hours volunteering to spread the message of FIRST throughout our neighborhood during the 2017-2018 season. We have volunteered in Governor’s Island with Bloomberg and also worked with them to host a coat drive, where we were able to collect 198 coats in our school! Last year, we partnered with a local elementary school to start an after-school robotics program to expose more children to topics related to STEM and give them hands-on experience. Our reach expands far beyond our school’s walls. Twice a year, we host a book sale in our TriBeCa neighborhood and we are now a familiar presence, demonstrating our robots to the many interested parents and children. We are so ubiquitous in the neighborhood that in 2014 we were even seen on the corner ofChambers and Greenwich Street on Google Maps! Our team, in partnership with other school clubs, has also collected and donated over 1.5tons of food to homeless shelters in the New York Area.
