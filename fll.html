2017-2018 - Mentoring

Our Work

Last year, we worked with a local elementary school (P.S.124) to create an afterschool program, teaching kids about LEGO robotics. This year we have expanded that to create and mentor two FLL teams. Our members currently mentor 15 FLL teams. We also have formed other FTC and FLL teams by working with programs like RoboMindTech and Forest Hills Robotics.
